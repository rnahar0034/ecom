What is an X-Ray ?

An X-ray (radiograph) is a special image that uses low dose radiation to create pictures of bones and other internal tissues such as your lungs and bowel. Qscan uses state of the art digital X-ray equipment, resulting in a reduced amount of radiation for excellent image quality.
