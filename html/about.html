About us

Since 2006, Qscan Radiology Clinics has grown to become one of Queensland’s largest, independently owned and operated diagnostic medical imaging providers, providing the Sunshine State with a wide range of radiology services including Xray, Ultrasound, CT, MRI, Nulclear Medicine, Bone Mineral Density, OPG Dental, Interventional Radiology and other specialised health services.

Qscan offer state-wide service delivery through 19 locations throughout Queensland including Annerley, Aspley, Brisbane City, Burleigh Waters, Cleveland, Everton Park, Kedron, Mater Private Clinic, Mermaid Waters, Redcliffe, Southport, Toowong, Victoria Point and Windsor.
